About Our Mission

Our Mission

At Veggie Delights, we believe that good health starts with the produce on your plate. Our mission is to empower consumers with the knowledge to:

  1. Pick peak‑fresh vegetables.
  2. Store them for maximum shelf life.
  3. Understand their nutritional benefits.
  4. We partner with local and sustainable farms to bring you seasonal highlights each month.

Produce Guide

Spring & Summer Favorites

  • Asparagus: Rich in folate and vitamin K—best from April to June.
  • Broccoli: High in fiber and vitamin C—peak in summer months.

Quick Seasonal Tip

Seasonal Spotlight:
Roasted Brussels sprouts with a squeeze of lemon are at their best November through December—try
tossing them with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of sea salt!

Fall & Winter Selections

  • Brussels Sprouts: Excellent source of vitamin A and C—harvested from October through December.
  • Winter Squash: Packed with beta‑carotene and potassium—enjoy from November to February.

Storage Best Practices

Refrigerate leafy greens: Keep between 32–40°F in high‑humidity drawer.

Room‑temperature roots: Store carrots and beets in a cool, dark place for up to 2 weeks.
